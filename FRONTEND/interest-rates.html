💡 How Interest Rates Affect Exchange Rates

  • Higher interest rates attract foreign investment, increasing demand for the local currency and typically strengthening it against others.
  • When a central bank cuts rates, the currency often weakens as investors seek higher returns elsewhere — this directly impacts exchange rates in Egypt.
  • The spread between Egypt's rate (27.25%) and the US rate (4.50%) is a key driver of EGP/USD dynamics — a wider spread can support the Egyptian pound.
