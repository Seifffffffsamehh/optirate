📖 Understanding Market Terms (أنواع السوق ومستوى المخاطر)
📈 Bull Market (سوق صاعد)
Prices are rising over time. Generally a good time to sell foreign currency or hold assets that are increasing in value.
📉 Bear Market (سوق هابط)
Prices are falling over time. Could be a good opportunity to buy foreign currency at lower rates before a potential rebound.
➖ Sideways Market (سوق عرضي)
Prices move within a narrow range without a clear upward or downward trend. Lower risk, but also lower opportunity for significant gains.
🟢 Low Risk (مخاطر منخفضة)
The market is stable with low volatility. Predictions are more reliable and sudden price swings are unlikely.
🟡 Medium Risk (مخاطر متوسطة)
Some price fluctuation is expected. Predictions carry moderate uncertainty — proceed with caution.
🔴 High Risk (مخاطر عالية)
The market is highly volatile and unpredictable. Significant price swings possible — only act if you can tolerate potential losses.
